# SME Investment Readiness Platform - Example Interaction Scenario

## Overview

This document illustrates how the **Administrator**, **SME User**, and **Investor** interact within the platform to move a startup from "interested" to "investment-ready."

---

## 1. The Setup (Admin Role)

**Persona:** Sarah, Program Manager at TechHub Accelerator.

**Goal:** Launch the "AgriTech 2024" Batch to find promising startups.

**Actions:**

1.  **Framework Config:** Sarah updates the **Assessment** questions to increase the weight of "Sustainability Impact" for this batch.
    - _(Admin Dashboard -> Question Builder)_
2.  **Program Creation:** She creates a new program called "AgriTech 2024" and opens it for enrollment.
    - _(Admin Dashboard -> Programs -> Create)_
3.  **Invite:** She sends an email blast inviting startups to register.

---

## 2. The Assessment (SME Role)

**Persona:** Sokha, Founder of "GreenData Farm Solutions."

**Goal:** Secure $50k seed funding to expand operations.

**Actions:**

1.  **Registration:** Sokha registers her company on the platform.
2.  **Initial Assessment:** She logs in to her **SME Dashboard**.
    - She sees her Readiness Score is currently **0/100**.
    - She clicks "Start Assessment" and answers questions across the 8 pillars.
3.  **The Result:** She gets a score of **65/100**.
    - **Strong:** Market Validation (80/100).
    - **Weak:** Financial Governance (40/100) - Missing audited financials.
4.  **Taking Action:**
    - The dashboard recommends: "Hire an external auditor."
    - Sokha creates a **Goal**: "Complete 2023 Financial Audit by Q3."
    - _(SME Dashboard -> Goals)_

---

## 3. The Discovery (Investor Role)

**Persona:** James, Angel Investor at Mekong Capital.

**Goal:** Find high-potential AgriTech startups with solid market traction.

**Actions:**

1.  **Search:** James logs in to his **Investor Dashboard**.
2.  **Filter:** He filters for:
    - Sector: "AgriTech"
    - Readiness Score: "> 60"
    - Market Validation: "> 75"
3.  **Review:** "GreenData Farm Solutions" appears in the results.
4.  **Deep Dive:** He clicks on GreenData's profile.
    - He sees the strong market score.
    - He notices the low financial score but sees the **Active Goal**: "Complete 2023 Financial Audit."
5.  **Engagement:**
    - James clicks "Watch" to follow their progress.
    - He sends a message through the platform: "Reach out once your audit is done; your market numbers look great."

---

## 4. Closing the Loop (Success)

**Outcome:**

1.  **SME:** Sokha uploads her audit report 2 months later. Her score jumps to **78/100**.
2.  **Notification:** James (Investor) gets a notification: "GreenData updated Financial Readiness score."
    - _(Preferences -> Notification Settings)_
3.  **Deal:** James schedules a meeting, knowing the main risk (financials) is now mitigated.
